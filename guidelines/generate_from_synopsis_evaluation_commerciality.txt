1. Grade the script commerciality as Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor.

- How marketable is the concept and to what demographic? 
- Classify the estimated budget into one of Low (up to $5M), Medium ($5-50M), High (above $50M)? Do we need special effects? A famous cast?
